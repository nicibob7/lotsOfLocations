Music

Uruguay has a rich musical heritage that spans various genres and styles. Traditional Uruguayan music is deeply influenced by European, African, and indigenous cultures.

Candombe

Candombe is a traditional Afro-Uruguayan music and dance style that originated in Uruguay, particularly in Montevideo, the capital city. It has its roots in the African slave communities that were brought to Uruguay during the colonial era, mainly from West Africa. Candombe is an important cultural and musical expression of Afro-Uruguayan identity.
